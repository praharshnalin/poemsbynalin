In India's tale of glory, a leader so great,
Atal Bihari Bajpayee, faced every state.

With bravery and smarts, he led the way,
His words were like music, calming dismay.

Through tough times, he kept us united,
with wisdom and courage, he never felt frightened.

He showed how to stand tall and strong,
in his leadership we all belong.

In every challenge, he found a solution,
a true hero for our nation's evolution.

In times of trouble, he didn't flee.
With intergrity shining, for all to see.

A leader strong, with a heart so true,
Atal Bihari Vajpayee, we salute you.

Praharsh Nalin
